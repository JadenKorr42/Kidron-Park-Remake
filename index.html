Welcome to the
Kidron Park

The Kidron Park is located in Downtown Kidron, Ohio. It is 4 miles south of Route 30 and 2 miles north of Route 250. The nearest RV park is Evergreen Park RV Resort in Mt. Eaton.

Our facilities include: 2 Ball Diamonds, a Concession Stand, a Picnic Pavilion, Playground Equipment, a Recreation Building, a Community Building and Walking Paths in our woods.

The Community Building is a perfect place to have all kinds of events including: weddings, family reunions, company banquets, graduation parties, and church picnics. The Recreational Building has volley ball, basketball, and tennis.

The availability of our facilities can be found on our calendar. (The Recreation Building schedule is not listed. Please contact us for its availability.)
