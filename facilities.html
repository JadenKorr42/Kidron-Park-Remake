Rental Information

  1. A deposit ($50) must be made within 10 days of receipt of statement. Total payment due two weeks prior to the event. Two statements will be mailed out. Please sign one and mail it back with payment. The other is for your records.
  2. NO Alcoholic Beverages are permitted at the Park.
  3. NO Smoking in the Community Building.
  4. NO Auction of any kind can take place at the Park on Sundays.
  5. If you cancel the event, send statement with date cancelled to P.O. Box 322, Kidron, Ohio 44636
  6. In case of cancellation or a no-show, the deposit will be considered a donation to the Park. A deposit cannot be carried over to a new date.
  7. No one can dominate a date each year unless approved by the Park trustees.
  8. When renting the Community Building, before leaving you must clean the building, remove the trash, clean and put away any kitchen supplies that were used and take everything you brought with you. You cannot leave food in the refrigerator or freezer.
  9. When renting the Pavilion, you must bag all trash and take it to the dumpster.
  10. When renting the Ball Fields, all paper, pop cans and trash must be thrown away in the provided trash cans or the dumpster.
  11. An additional fee may be required when renting facilities to cover cleaning costs if necessary.
  12. In the buildings, you are not permitted to hang anything on the walls or ceiling except where hooks are provided.
  13. Any damaged window candles in the Community Building will need to be replaced at your expense.
  14. On the upper floor of the Community Building, No food or drinks are permitted and children must be supervised.

Community Building Information

  • The Community Building is a 63ft x 98ft facility.
  • It has heating and air conditioning and is available year round.
  • It has a kitchen which includes: a stove, confectionery oven, refrigerator,
    freezer, coffee pots and chaffing dishes.
  • There are indoor bathrooms.
  • There are 30 5-foot round tables, 30 8-foot rectangular tables and 400 chairs.
  • Max Occupancy: 454 People
